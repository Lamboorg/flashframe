ğŸ§  Spaced Repetition System (SRS)

What is SRS?

SRS is a scientifically-proven learning technique that shows you cards at optimal intervals. Cards you know well appear less frequently, while difficult cards appear more often. This maximizes retention while minimizing study time.

How It Works

After viewing a card, rate how well you knew it:

  • Again - Didn't know it. See again in 1 minute.
  • Hard - Struggled. Short interval increase.
  • Good - Knew it with some effort. Normal interval.
  • Easy - Knew it instantly. Larger interval boost.

New cards start in the learning phase with short intervals (1min â†’ 10min). Once you pass these steps, the card "graduates" to the review phase where intervals grow (1 day â†’ 3 days â†’ 1 week â†’ etc). If you forget a card, it goes back to relearning.
